Belice

Belize , with a population of approximately 380,000 inhabitants, is located on the northern Caribbean coast of Central America. Its capital is the city of Belmopan and the most populated city is Belize City . The encounter between the Caribbean Indians and the Africans who were brought to Belize as slaves by the European colonizers, gave rise to a new ethnic group: the Garifuna . Belize was part of the Mayan Empire that stretched from southern Mexico to Guatemala and Honduras. Later it was explored by the Spanish and later colonized by the British Empire until its full independence in 1981. Belize is part of the important Mesoamerican biological corridor, with great biological diversity, both marine and terrestrial. The Caribbean coast is lined with coral reefs and some 450 islets and islands known locally as ' cayes '. Three of the four coral reefs in the western hemisphere are located off the coast of Belize . It is the only country in Central America where the official language is English , being a former British colony, although Spanish, Belizean Creole, and to a lesser extent, Mayan, Arawak and Garifuna are also spoken. Bilingualism is very common and encouraged. The majority of the population professes Catholicism, and British influence has brought a considerable and varied Protestant congregation, including German and Swiss Mennonites.

Guatemala

Located in the northwestern corner of Central America, the Republic of Guatemala has an approximate population of 15,700,000 inhabitants, and a wide indigenous culture, product of the Mayan heritage and Spanish influence during the colonial period. Its capital is Guatemala City , officially called "Nueva Guatemala de la Asunción". Despite its relatively small territorial extension, Guatemala has a great climatic variety, product of its mountainous relief , reaching a height of up to 4,220 meters above sea level. The official language is Spanish , although it is used as a second language by some ethnic groups in other parts of the country. There are alsoTwenty-one Mayan languages , in addition to the Xinca and Garifuna languages, the latter spoken by the Afro-descendant population in the Caribbean department of Izabal. The current Guatemalan culture is the result of the numerous Mayan, Spanish, Afro-Caribbean, Mexican and to a lesser extent American influences , as well as the rest of America. The influence of the natives and the Spanish colonists can still be seen throughout Guatemala. "Typical" fabrics and some traditional clothing are made in a traditional Mayan way, however, cross-culturalization has had an influence on clothing, mainly in the young indigenous population. Important archaeological sites with Mayan ruins can also be found .

El Salvador

Located on the Pacific coast of Central America, the Republic of El Salvador , with a population of approximately 6.5 million inhabitants, is the smallest country in Central America and the most densely populated in the entire American continent. Its capital is San Salvador , the largest city in the country. Known as the Land of Volcanoes , El Salvador is full of ancestral traditions and customs from pre-Hispanic cultures that merged with Spanish customs.
Territorial Expansion: 21,041 Km2.
Departments: Ahuachapán, Cabañas, Chalatenango, Cuscatlán, La Libertad, La Paz, La Unión, Morazán, San Miguel, San Salvador, San Vicente, Santa Ana, Sonsonate, Usulután.

Honduras

The Republic of Honduras , located in the center-north of Central America, has a population of approximately 8.7 million inhabitants. Its capital is Tegucigalpa . The territory of Honduras is very rugged, formed by high rows of mountains, high plains, and deep valleys where there are extensive and fertile plains crossed by mighty rivers which contributes to its rich biodiversity. Its privileged location between two oceans and its topographic conditions create a wide variety of habitats, from cloud forests to coral reefs , favorable for a high diversity of flora and fauna. Honduras is a multi-ethnic, multicultural and multilingual countrywith four great ethnic families: the whites or mestizos who are the majority, the indigenous peoples (Lencas, Misquitos, Tolupanes, Chortis, Pech, Tawahkas), the Garifuna and the English-speaking Creoles. Although the official language of Honduras is Spanish, the Constitution is committed to preserving and promoting native cultures.
Territorial Expansion: 112,492Km2.
Departments: Atlántida, Colón, Comayagua, Copán, Cortés, Choluteca, El Paraíso, Francisco Morazán, Gracias a Dios, Intibucá, Islase la Bahía, La Paz, Lempira, Ocotepeque, Olancho, Santa Bárbara, Valle Yoro.

Nicaragua

Located in the geographic center of the Central American isthmus, the Republic of Nicaragua has a population of multi-ethnic nature estimated at 6.3 million inhabitants. Its capital is Managua . Inhabited by pre-Columbian peoples , the Pacific Ocean coast and part of the central region of the current territory of Nicaragua was conquered by the Spanish, while British colonial rule was established on the Caribbean coast . Nicaragua is a volcanic and tropical country , housing two large lakes in its interior: Lake Xolotlán and Lake Cocibolca or «Great Lake of Nicaragua». Its privileged location makes the country a hostel ofvast biodiversity . The Spanish is the official language , but are also recognized indigenous languages as English native peoples Nicaraguan Creole, Miskito, Sumo and Sumo, Mayagna, Garifuna and Rama . Religion is an important part of Nicaraguan culture, with Catholicism being the predominant religious denomination, followed by Protestantism.
Territorial Expansion: 129,999Km2.
Departments: Boaco, Carazo, Chinandega, Chontales, Costa Caribe Norte, Costa Caribe Sur, Estelí, Granada, Jinotega, León, Madriz, Managua, Masaya, Matagalpa, Nueva Segovia, Río San Juan, Rivas.

Costa Rica

The Republic of Costa Rica has an estimated population of 4.8 million inhabitants. Its capital, political and economic center is San José . The territory of Costa Rica is a narrow strip of land surrounded by two oceans, and is the country with the greatest biodiversity on the planet per square kilometer of territory . It is a very mountainous country and most of the territory is made up of elevations between 900 and 1800 meters above sea level. Costa Rican culture has indigenous, European, Afro-Caribbean and Asian influences, being the predominant religion Christianity. Its cultural features have been marked by its position on the isthmus, as a bridge between two great continental masses, generating a line of passage for all kinds of currents and influences. Although Spanish is the official language , five indigenous languages ​​are also spoken: Maleku, Cabécar, Bribri, Guaymí and Bocotá . Mekatelyu is spoken in the Caribbean area of ​​the country, it is what is academically known as Limonean Creole English.
Territorial Expansion: 51,100Km2.
Departments: San José, Alajuela, Cartago, Heredia, Guanacaste, Puntarenas, Limón.

Panamá

Located in the southeast of Central America, the Republic of Panama has a population of more than four million inhabitants. More than 70% of Panamanians live in urban areas, and half of them in Panama City, its capital . Panama is the southernmost country and one of the most biodiverse , consisting mostly of lowlands. Its status as a transit country made it an early meeting point for cultures from around the world. The country is the geographical setting of the Panama Canal , a work that facilitates communication between the coasts of the Atlantic and Pacific oceans . The Spanish is the official language of the country and also recognized as indigenous official languages in the ngäbe, Bugle, Kuna, Embera, Wounaan, Naso Tjerdi and bri bri . English is the foreign language of greatest use and demand, and is spoken by Afro-Antillians from the country's Atlantic coast, and in Panama City. Due to its cultural diversity, a wide range of religions are practiced in the country ; however, the Catholic religion is the predominant one.
Territorial Expansion: 75,517Km2.
Departments: Coclé, Colón, Chiriquí, Fábrega, Herrera, Los Santos, Panamá.
