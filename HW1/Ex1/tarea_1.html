The series is about the adventures of an unusual detective, a holistic detective. That is, instead of resorting to methods usual research, Dirk Gently used quantum physics and the improbable to reveal how even the most impossible clues and pilgrims could be related to each other. In this way, their adventures ran through scenarios that transcended space, the time and logic and reason. Although, well seen, everything ended up having a consistent and plausible explanation. Dirk Gently is an absurd, crazy and at times pointless comedy that is very funny.
